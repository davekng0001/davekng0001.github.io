Ambisonic Microphone




About Project


  • For my senior design project I created an ambisonic microphone, which is a spatial 360 degree microphone. The audio recorded can be encoded with spatial data through an algorithm applied with software thanks to the the arrangement of the microphone capsules(LFU,RFD,RBU,LBD), and the microphones sensitivity pattern.

Future Goals For Project

  • Also would like to encase mic and components in a better structure.





Recording Setup


  • I created a preamp circuit to amplify the the raw audio from the 4 micropone capsules. The 4 signals were sent to a mic interface which sent it to my computer where the audio was saved. This audio was than operated on by a matlab script to encode spatial data into it. I arranged the script to output stereo audio.
